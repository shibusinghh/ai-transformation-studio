# Demo Run — Change-Readiness Diagnostic

**Input:** `sample-survey.csv` — a 10-respondent pulse survey from a fictional mid-size firm (post-restructuring, one abandoned chatbot pilot). Small sample by design: the demo shows the scoring discipline, including what the tool refuses to score.

## Executive summary

**Overall band: NOT READY (25-49).** The two weakest dimensions are governance & risk maturity (25) and workforce sentiment & trust (30). The single biggest risk: a mandatory rollout into a workforce that reads AI through the lens of last year's restructuring would convert the license budget into distrust. A rollout here should be opt-in cohorts behind published guarantees, with governance basics built first.

*All scores are capped at "indicative": n=10 is a single small sample. Re-score on the full population before any investment decision.*

## Top risks (from the weak dimensions)

| Risk | L×I | Mitigation | Tripwire |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trust collapse on mandate (restructuring memory + monitoring fear) | H×H | Opt-in cohorts; published no-displacement and telemetry-exclusion guarantees | Trust item drops below 2.5 or fear rises above 4.0 in any wave group → pause expansion |
| Shadow AI spreads while approvals stay opaque | H×M | Inventory with amnesty in 30 days; visible fast-lane approval SLA | Second shadow deployment found after amnesty closes → shutdown-first policy activates |
| Works council escalation (unanswered questions on record) | M×H | Respond formally within 2 weeks; joint committee before design freeze | Formal dispute filed → market pause and negotiated restart |
| Training theater repeats (webinar ≠ capability) | M×M | Tiered, practice-based literacy program with evidence logging | <60% of wave-1 users pass the practical check → redesign before wave 2 |
| Pilot death repeats and cements cynicism | M×H | Public post-mortem of the 2024 chatbot before anything launches | Any new pilot passes its gate date without a kill/promote decision → steering escalation |

## Recommended 90 days (mapped to the six-move sequence)

1. **Weeks 1-4 — Move 1:** AI use inventory with amnesty (the IT shadow pilot comes in from the cold); answer the works council letter; public post-mortem of the 2024 chatbot.
2. **Weeks 3-8 — Move 2:** guarantees drafted, signed, published; leadership narrative agreed (one story, told by named executives, acknowledging the restructuring).
3. **Weeks 6-12 — Move 3 start:** pick two workflows maximum, name owners, map them; collect the missing change-capacity data (manager interviews, program portfolio).
4. **Re-score at day 90.** Target: governance and trust both above 50 before any mandatory rollout is even scheduled.

## Data gaps to close before re-scoring

Full-population survey; manager-level interviews (capacity + cascade readiness); program portfolio inventory; works council positions on record; literacy evidence audit (Article 4 if EU).
